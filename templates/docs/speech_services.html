Speech Services

Available Services

Asynchronous Processing

All speech services use async job processing for reliability and scalability. Submit your request, receive a job ID, then poll for completion. Perfect for large files and high-volume processing.

Authentication required: Include your token in the X-Token header for all requests.

Speech-to-Text
Basic Transcription
Convert audio recordings to accurate text using Microsoft's advanced speech recognition technology. Perfect for quick transcription needs.
  • High-accuracy transcription
  • Multiple audio formats (WAV, MP3, M4A, FLAC, OGG)
  • Multiple language support
  • Profanity filtering
  • Fast async processing

How It Works

Audio to Text
Speech-to-Text & Diarization Workflow
1

Upload Audio File

Upload your audio file using POST /file and receive a file_id

2

Submit Job

Send file_id to POST /speech/stt or POST /speech/stt_diarize

3

Monitor Progress

Poll GET /jobs/status with your job_id to check completion

4

Retrieve Results

Get transcription from GET /jobs/result when status is "completed"

Text to Audio
Text-to-Speech Workflow
1

Submit Text

Send text, voice selection, and format to POST /speech/tts

2

Processing

GAIA processes your text with Microsoft's neural voice synthesis

3

Check Status

Monitor job progress using GET /jobs/status

4

Download Audio

Retrieve audio file_id from GET /jobs/result then download the audio

Processing Times & Best Practices

Speech-to-Text: Typically 20-50% of audio length (e.g., 10-minute audio = 2-5 minutes processing)

Diarization: Additional 30-60 seconds for AI speaker identification

Text-to-Speech: Usually 10-30 seconds regardless of text length

Polling: Check status every 5-10 seconds for optimal balance between responsiveness and API efficiency

Voice Selection Tips

Choose voices based on your target audience and content type:

  • Content type: Professional voices for business, friendly voices for customer service
  • Gender considerations: Some studies suggest female voices for instructional content, male voices for authoritative content

Technical Specifications

Audio Input Requirements
For Speech-to-Text Services
  • Formats: WAV, MP3, M4A, FLAC, OGG
  • Max file size: 200MB
  • Max duration: 2 hours per file
  • Recommended sample rate: 16kHz or higher
  • Channels: Mono or stereo
  • Best results: Clear audio, minimal background noise
Text Input & Output
For Text-to-Speech Service
  • Max text length: 10,000 characters
  • Output formats: MP3 (16kHz, 32kbps/128kbps), WAV/PCM
  • Voice options: 8 regional voice personas
  • Languages: English (US, GB, AU, ZA)
  • Audio quality: Neural voice synthesis
  • Processing: Async for reliability
Job Management
All Services
  • Status tracking: pending → processing → completed/failed
  • Job retention: Results available for 24 hours
  • Polling frequency: Recommended 5-10 seconds
  • Timeout handling: No client timeouts with async
  • Error reporting: Detailed error messages
  • Retry logic: Built-in for transient failures
